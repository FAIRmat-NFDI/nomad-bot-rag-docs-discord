Forum
NOMAD hosts a forum on matsci.org together with many other materials science projects. NOMAD developers and users will be able to help you here.
bug_report
Open an Issue
You can open an issue on NOMAD's main GitHub project. This way, we can track your problem and start implementing a solution.
General Inquiries
If the other help channels are not appropriate for your problem—for example, if you have a general inquiry or need non-technical support—feel free to contact us via email. Please note that response times are typically longer than for the other channels.
Professional Deployment and Customization Services
Looking for support with implementing NOMAD Oasis at the institutional or organizational level? Our official partner, glAIde Data, can help your organization to efficiently establish a tailored and robust data management platform.
